AccidentGuard - Bill of Materials (BOM)
=========================================

All prices are approximate in Indian Rupees (INR).

Core Components
---------------
1. ESP32 DevKit V1 (38-pin or 30-pin)
   - Quantity: 1
   - Price: 350-500 INR
   - Note: Any ESP32 with WiFi/BLE works

2. ADXL345 Accelerometer Module (GY-291)
   - Quantity: 1
   - Price: 120-180 INR
   - Note: Already has voltage regulator and I2C pullups on board

3. NEO-6M GPS Module (with ceramic antenna)
   - Quantity: 1
   - Price: 400-600 INR
   - Note: Comes with antenna, use in open sky for best results

4. Active Buzzer (5V, 85dB or higher)
   - Quantity: 1
   - Price: 20-50 INR
   - Note: Active buzzer needs no tone library, just HIGH/LOW

5. High-Brightness LED (5mm White or 10mm)
   - Quantity: 1-3
   - Price: 5-20 INR
   - Note: For flashlight effect, use 10000mcd or higher

6. Resistors (330 ohm for LED, 1K for transistor base)
   - Quantity: 5
   - Price: 10 INR total

7. NPN Transistor 2N2222 or BC547 (for buzzer if needed)
   - Quantity: 1
   - Price: 5-10 INR

Optional Components
-------------------
8. SIM800L GSM Module (for SMS and calling)
   - Quantity: 1
   - Price: 350-500 INR
   - Note: Requires SIM card and separate 4V power supply

9. TP4056 LiPo Charger Module
   - Quantity: 1
   - Price: 30-60 INR

10. 3.7V LiPo Battery (2000mAh or higher)
    - Quantity: 1
    - Price: 250-400 INR

11. MT3608 Boost Converter (to get 5V from LiPo)
    - Quantity: 1
    - Price: 30-50 INR

12. ABS Project Box (100x60x25mm)
    - Quantity: 1
    - Price: 80-150 INR

Miscellaneous
-------------
- Jumper wires (male-to-male, male-to-female): 50 INR
- Breadboard (for prototyping): 60-80 INR
- PCB or perfboard (for final build): 30-50 INR
- USB Micro cable: 50-100 INR
- Small metal bolt or coin (touch pad): available at home
- Soldering iron and solder: if not owned, 300-600 INR

Total Estimated Cost
--------------------
Minimum (no SIM, no battery): 1000-1500 INR
With SIM800L and LiPo battery: 1700-2500 INR
